This page informs you of our data privacy policies.

Website Tracking

We do not perform any user tracking on our websites.

Web Server Logs

Our webserver stores connection logs for purely technical reasons to ensure correct functional operation of the website. These are automatically deleted after a maximum of 31 days.

Google Maps Embeds

Our site uses map embeds from google. The map embeds are required for correct operation of our website. Google's data privacy policy can be found here: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en&gl=en

Changes To This Privacy Policy

Gamecamp Munich may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Site. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
